Site Title

"B&D" or "Beginners & Dungeons"

Site Purpose

This site provides a general resource for new players to come and learn how to play the table top game "Dungeons & Dragons" or "D&D" for short. Some starting tips and tricks will be given as well as some general tools for gameplay such as dice rollers, monster generators, and the like. The goal of this page is to give the user a better understanding of this tabletop game and to encourage creativity as new players become more involved.
