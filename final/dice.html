Roll The Dice

In Dungeons & Dragons, dice are used to determine the outcomes of actions, like attacks, skill checks, and saving throws. Each type of die (like d20, d6, etc.) represents different aspects of the game, with the d20 being the most common for resolving challenges. The randomness of dice adds excitement and unpredictability, making each game session unique. They also help balance the game, as player success depends on both strategy and chance, fostering creativity and storytelling within the gameplay.
